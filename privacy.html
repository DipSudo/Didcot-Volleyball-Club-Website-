Privacy Notice for [Sport Club Name]

At [Sport Club Name] (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you interact with us, whether as a member, participant, volunteer, or visitor to our club.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, phone number, email address, and date of birth.
  • Membership Information: Membership status, payment details, registration forms, and participation history.
  • Health Information: Medical conditions or health-related details necessary for participation in sports activities (with your explicit consent).
  • Payment Information: Payment methods, transaction details, and membership fees.
  • Communications: Records of your interactions with us, such as emails, messages, or phone calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To manage and maintain your membership.
  • To facilitate registration for sports activities, events, and competitions.
  • To communicate important club updates, events, or changes in schedules.
  • To process payments and manage financial transactions.
  • To ensure health and safety standards by managing medical information (with consent).
  • To improve club services and tailor our offerings to your needs.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers that help us manage our operations, such as payment processors and IT service providers.
  • Sports Authorities: Relevant sports governing bodies when required for event participation, registrations, or compliance purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: Authorities or legal bodies where required by law or to protect the safety of our members.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain conditions.
  • Portability: Request that we transfer your data to another organization in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
