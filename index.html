About Us

Didcot Volleyball Club is a community-based club welcoming players of all ages, gender, and skill levels to join.

Established in 2001 in the heart of Didcot, Oxfordshire, Didcot Volleyball Club is committed to fostering the sport of volleyball. We provide training sessions, friendly matches, and social events for enthusiasts of all levels. Our social sessions offer a great chance to meet new people, stay active, and enjoy the game in a friendly atmosphere.
