About Us

Didcot Volleyball Club is a community-based club welcoming players of all ages, gender, and skill levels to join.

Our mission is to promote the sport of volleyball in the local community and provide a fun and inclusive environment for players to learn and grow. We offer training sessions, friendly matches, and social events for enthusiasts of all levels. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player looking to compete, we have something for everyone. Join us today and be part of the club!

Resources for Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport that is both exciting and fun. It's a great way to stay fit and socialize, but can be daunting at first if you have never played before. Here are some resources that can help you understand the game.

Beginner

Learn the basics of volleyball, including rules, positions, and fundamental skills.

Intermediate

Build on your foundational skills with more complex drills, tactical strategies, and techniques to elevate your game.

Advanced

Master the game with expert-level tactics, conditioning, and professional insights.

Equipment

Discover the essential gear you need to play volleyball, from balls to knee pads and shoes.
